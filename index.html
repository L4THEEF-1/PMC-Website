WELCOME TO PMC

A Malaysian streetwear storytelling brand paying homage to the things we find
familiar whilst growing up in Southeast Asia. Using apparel as a canvas for stories,

COLLEGE CAT

First introduced in 2022, College Cat quickly became a staple in our PMC design
arsenal, perfectly capturing our love for stylish cats. Over the years, we've unveiled
various iterations of College Cat across numerous silhouettes, each one
embodying the playful yet sophisticated spirit of this beloved character.
